Representation, Inclusion & Impact

We are committed to inclusion, both in front of and behind the camera.

Young Adults on the Autism Spectrum

My eldest son, RK Jr., is on the autism spectrum. For nearly two decades, he's thrived at Pathpoint, Inc., a program supporting neurodiverse adults. During production of ONE NIGHT IN LA, RK became fascinated by the filmmaking process ‚Äî and I saw his light ignite.

Because of him, I've created a program that brings 5 young adults with autism onto our film crew. They'll contribute to wardrobe, lighting, set design, and production support ‚Äî after completing a custom filmmaking curriculum designed to meet them where they are.
